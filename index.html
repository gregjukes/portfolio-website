Bucket Drumming with Kadence Arts

Bucket Drumming with Kadence Arts is a case study and proposal for UX research based on a series of educational bucks drumming videos I created for Boston-based arts nonprofit, Kadence Arts to bring music to summer camps facing extraordinary programming restrictions in summer 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. I managed a team of four other people for this project; co-created the educational content; scripted, directed, and edited the videos; and managed distribution.

This portfolio piece focuses on UX research and user testing skills applied to the project.
