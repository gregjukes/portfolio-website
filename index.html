Memory Tour is a collaborative scavenger hunt web application I designed as a student project.

My work encompassed the full spectrum of UX design including market surveys, user research, creating personas, information architecture, iterative design, prototyping, and testing.

Bucket Drumming with Kadence Arts

Bucket Drumming with Kadence Arts is a case study and proposal for UX research based on a series of educational bucket drumming videos I created for Boston-based arts nonprofit, Kadence Arts to bring music to summer camps facing extraordinary programming restrictions in summer 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. I managed a team of four other people for this project; co-created the educational content; scripted, directed, and edited the videos; and managed distribution.

This portfolio piece focuses on UX research and user testing skills applied to the project.
